Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

Who we are

Azadi Financial Services is a trading name of Azadi Motors Limited (registered in England No. 1770010). We are the data controller for the personal information we collect through this portal.

Information we collect

  • Identity data: name, date of birth
  • Contact data: address, email, phone number
  • Financial data: agreement details, payment history, bank details (encrypted at rest)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, session identifiers

How we use your information

  • To manage your finance agreement and process payments
  • To verify your identity when you log in
  • To send transactional communications (payment confirmations, settlement figures)
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the basis of:

  • Contract: processing necessary to perform our agreement with you
  • Legal obligation: processing required by financial regulations
  • Legitimate interest: fraud prevention and security

Data sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Stripe: to process card payments (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Cloud Platform: to host and store data securely
  • Regulatory bodies: where required by law

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data retention

We retain your data for the duration of your agreement plus six years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements. Audit logs are retained for three years.

Your rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

Security

We protect your data using encryption at rest and in transit, content security policies, rate limiting, session management, and audit logging. Bank details are AES-256 encrypted — only the last four digits of your account number are ever displayed.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, write to the Data Protection Officer at Azadi Financial Services, Imaginary Road, Birmingham B28 0HB or call 0345 786 4321.
