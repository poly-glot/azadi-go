Ways to Pay

Direct Debit

Direct Debit is the easiest way to pay. Your payments are automatically collected from your bank account on your chosen payment date each month.

If you haven't already set up a Direct Debit, please contact our customer services team on 0345 786 4321 and we'll arrange this for you.

Debit Card

You can make a one-off payment using your debit card through our online payment facility.

Alternatively, call our customer services team on 0345 786 4321 to make a payment over the phone.
